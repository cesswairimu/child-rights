Youth Talk Africa is a community based organization which its main objective is to address social economical and health needs for the youth in east africa.

<
Motivation

Almost half of the world's population is younger than 30 years of age. 70 percent live in developing countries.

So?

Adolescents are the children of tomorrow. Their choices regarding education strongly influence the development of their country and its society

Therefore

The transfer of knowledge and education in sexual and reproductive health and rights help them make informed and autonomous decisions.

Youth talk africa promotes youth physically, mentally, emotionally and culturally and many more other ways.

Our goal is ...

To motivate the biggest bracket of people in the world that is the youth to grab opportunities when they present themselves. In so doing they can establish a firm foundation of their future and their country at large.

To encourage the youth to expand their knowledge in both education and social activities so they can make informed decisions and be able to relate to people in their work places and also outside work life.

To teach youth about family planning and the importance of not entering into risky sexual relationships that might jeopardize their future. We also inform them about the dangers of drug misuse and how to avoid addictions.
